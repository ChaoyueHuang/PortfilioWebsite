OVERVIEW:

What is art? How will a humanbeing evaluate wether an art work is the one that motivate him? There is no difference in art, however, we can feel an art piece from our heart. But is it the only way for us to evaluate the influence of art works on people? When you say you are excited, sad or you love something, you are describing a kind of feeling. A feeling is actually the effect brought by the mix of several hormone and sometimes your feeling may deceive you. But there is a more direct way to catch your true reaction which is the EEG(Electroencephalography) signal.

Thus, we plan to apply this method to help people find the art piece that most touch him.

FLOOR PLAN & INSTALLATION DESIGN:

This installation is made for Shanghai Design Week 2019. We need to make use of the limited space and achieve the goal of creating an artistic experience.

There will be several sculptures and paintings surrounded by our exhibition area, so we can only make use of the central open space. We would like to bring an immersive experience to the audiences, the problem is generally it needs a quite large space to build an immersive scenes but we only have a few square meters of space. Our solution is building a wood box with a sliding door, which could bring up the freshness and mystery during the experience.
