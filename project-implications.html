IMPLICATIONS HAVE TRACES

2019.11
Generative art, Interactive Installation

TOOLS:

Processing/ Emotive

OVERVIEW:

An art work commission by The 2nd Shanghai International Digital Vision Exhibition.

In the Book of Changes, “德化情，情生意，意恒动。” How do you stop your mind in a fleeting moment? The work transforms the passage of thoughts in the mind into the strokes of the digital screen, to stop the moment of the intentional trace. The work uses brainwave sensing technology to read and detect four kinds of emotional changes of human beings, and connect dynamic information communication in the human brain with digital codes for visual expression. The picture generated by the work as a kind of digital memory is not only a means of preserving the past, but also a means of recycling the present and trying to shape the future.
