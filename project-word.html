WORD OF THE YEAR 2019

2019.12
Data visualization

TOOLS:

P5.JS

OVERVIEW:

2019 is about to pass. What words can best summarize the past year? At the end of each year, various institutions will launch rankings such as "Top Ten Hot Words / Top Ten Popular Words", while foreign authoritative dictionary agencies will select "word of the year". The Paper (www.thepaper.cn) has taken stock of various "2019 keywords" selected by various institutions. With the help of the Baidu index tool, it will take you to review the big and small things behind these words over the past year.

Link to the published article: Word of the year 2019

PROCESS:

Acquire Data

I got the weight value of how often each term is searched on Baidu pages from my colleague who crawled the web data.

Filter Data

The range of the raw data was quite large which will cause obstacle to the later visualization. We conducted the logarithmic operation so the index value was limited range without losing variation.

Represent Data

There are three goals for the vislization:

1. Reveal the data's variation along the year
2. Indicate the time when the data reach its highest value
3. Make it eaiser to tell the size of the data value

I refered the wind rose with a circle timeline as the basic method. So the readers could tell the data fluctuations and the time corresponding to the extreme point very easy. To make it easier for readers to tell the size of the data value, I applied the gradient color to correspond the size of the data value.
