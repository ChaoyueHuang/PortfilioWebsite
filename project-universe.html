ABOUT THE UNIVERSE

2018.11 - 2018.12
New media art, Creative coding

TOOLS:

Processing/ AdobePremiere/ AdobePhotoShop

BACKGROUND:

Living in this fast-paced socirty, people always put their eyes on the things around them, but ignore all the trivia in our world is nothing compared with the vast universe.

I built this project in order to remind people there are something etrnal and beautiful above our head. Meanwhile, I propose people to know more about the earth, space, and the universe and appreciate their beauty. It also has educational significance, I integrated some introductions about the planets. The project includes three parts, the earth and moon system, the solar system and the galaxy. The project is interactive and responsive by the mouse’s input.
